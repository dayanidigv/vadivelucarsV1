## **Design Philosophy:**

### **Core Principles**

1. **HUGE BUTTONS** - Easy to tap with greasy hands
2. **VOICE INPUT** - Speak part names instead of typing
3. **SMART TEMPLATES** - Remember common jobs
4. **PHOTO-FIRST** - Take damage photos easily
5. **ONE-TAP SHARE** - WhatsApp invoice instantly

---

## **Why This Matters**

This application is designed for mechanics working in real garage conditions:

- **Dirty Hands**: Large touch targets that work even with gloves
- **Noisy Environment**: Visual feedback over audio cues
- **Fast-Paced**: Minimize steps to complete common tasks
- **Mobile-First**: Designed for tablets and phones on the shop floor
- **Instant Communication**: Share invoices without leaving the app
